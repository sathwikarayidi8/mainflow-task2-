ABOUT

Hi there! I am Sathwika, the creator of Lavender Haazee. My story began when I was a kid wanting to make friendship bracelets and trade them with friends. A desire to create meaningful keepsakes.
I am passionate about creating beautiful and meaningful jewelry beyond just an accessory. Each piece is handcrafted with hands using traditional weaving methods and imbued with a touch of uniqueness. You can order and make customized jewelry for your loved ones or yourself.
We believe that jewelry can be a powerful symbol. Our collection features pieces designed based on current fashion trends with a touch of vintage designs. People of all age groups and genders can embrace our jewelry with joy.
